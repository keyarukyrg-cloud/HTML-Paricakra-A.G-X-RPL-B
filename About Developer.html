ABOUT THE GAME DEVELOPER

Behaviour Interactive is a large Canadian independent game developer and publisher, best known for its original title, Dead by Daylight. Founded in 1992,
the company has expanded internationally and is also a leading provider of external development services for major clients.
It has a reputation for fostering career growth and has received awards like "Best Places to Work in Canada" and
Deloitte's "Enterprise Fast 15" and "Best Managed Company" awards.
About Behaviour Interactive

  • Headquarters: Montreal, Canada
  • Other locations: Toronto, Canada; Seattle, USA; United Kingdom; and the Netherlands
  • Number of employees: Over 1,300 worldwide
  • Company structure: It is Canada's largest independent developer and publisher.
  • Financial backing: A minority stake in the company was acquired by Haveli Investments in 2022 to support continuous growth.
