About The Game

🩸 Overview

Dead by Daylight is an asymmetrical multiplayer horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive. It was first released in 2016, and has since become one of the most popular horror games worldwide.

In each match, five players enter the game:

•1 Killer 👹

•4 Survivors 🧍🧍‍♀️🧍🧍‍♂️

goal is simple but terrifying:

•The Killer hunts down and sacrifices Survivors to a dark entity known as The Entity.

•The Survivors must work together to repair 5 generators, open the exit gates, and escape — all while avoiding being caught.

⚙️ Gameplay Mechanics

🧍 Survivors:

•Play in third-person view for better awareness.

•Can repair generators, rescue teammates, heal, and hide.

•Must manage stealth and teamwork to survive.

•Can bring perks, items, and offerings to increase their chance of survival.

👹 Killers:

•Play in first-person view for immersion.

•Each Killer has unique powers and perks that affect their hunting style.

•Must catc Survivors, injure, hook, and eventually sacrifice them to The Entity.

🧠 Objectives

•Survivors: Repair 5 out of 7 generators → Open exit gates → Escape.

•Kller: Prevent escapes by catching and sacrificing all Survivors.
