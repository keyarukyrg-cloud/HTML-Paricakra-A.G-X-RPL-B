RECOMMENDED PERKS FOR THE LEGIONS

1.Thanatophobia



Thanatophobia is a perk in Dead by Daylight that slows down survivor actions when other survivors are injured, dying, or hooked.
It inflicts a stackable 1%/1.5%/2% penalty to repairing,sabotaging, and cleansing actions for each affected survivor,
up to a maximum of 4%/6%/8%. If all four survivors are affected, an additional 12% penalty is applied for a total of 16%/18%/20%.

! THIS PERKS WAS AN "THE NURSE" SIGNATURE PERKS !

2.Corrupt Intervention

Corrupt Intervention is a killer perk that blocks the three generators furthest from the
killer at the start of a trial. It deactivates prematurely once the first survivor is put into the dying state, but it provides an advantage
by forcing survivors to move towards the killer's spawn to find and repair generators.

! THIS PERKS WAS AN "THE PLAGUE" SIGNATURE PERKS !

3.Scourge Hook: Pain of Resonance

Scourge Hooks are special hooks that transform a few randomly selected hooks in a trial to have a unique effect, triggered by either hooking
a survivor on them or unhooking them from them. The specific effect depends on the killer's equipped Scourge Hook perk,
such as making the injured survivor's healing speed slower with Gift of Pain, or causing generators to explode with Pain Resonance.

! THIS PERKS WAS AN "ONRYO" SIGNATURE PERKS

4.Discordance

iscordance is a killer perk in Dead by Daylight that highlights any generator being repaired by two or more survivors within a specific range,
and triggers a loud noise notification when the generator is first highlighted. The highlighted yellow aura lingers for 4 seconds after
the generator is no longer being worked on by multiple survivors or is out of range.

! THIS PERKS WAS AN "THE LEGION" SIGNATURE PERKS !
