THE LEGION SKILL

POWER

Feral Frenzy

Enter a sprint and chain attacks to hit multiple Survivors. Upon hitting a Survivor in Feral Frenzy, your power gauge will be replenished, and the location of all Survivors within your Terror Radius will be revealed. While using Feral Frenzy, The Legion can vault over windows and pallets. Missing an attack, getting stunned, or running out of power will end Feral Frenzy.

PERKS

1.DISCORDANCE

2.MAD GRIT

3.IRON MAIDEN

ABOUT THE PERKS

1.DISCORDANCE

All generators being repaired by 2 or more Survivors are highlighted by a yellow aura.Receive a noise notification when cooperative repairs begin.

2.MAD GRIT

Attacks made while carrying Survivors have no cooldown. Be wary of crowding the Killer, as the consequences might be dire.

3.IRON MAIDEN

Fling lockers open with speed and purpose. Any Survivor exiting a locker will scream and becoming Exposed and vulnerable to one-hit downs for a duration.
